How to read this: Click a cover or any link to see the real listing, reviews, and synopsis on an outside site. Use “Synopsis” on a card to pull a web-sourced summary plus a neutral content note (sexual content, profanity, graphic violence, religious or political/identity themes) for that title — generated on demand, so verify anything borderline against the links. Theme tags are keyword guesses from the title only — they catch some relevant books and miss others, and will mislabel innocent ones (a title with “war” or “Christmas” trips them). They are a starting filter, not a judgment. Final fit for your district is your call; this tool gives you the information to make it. Duplicate flags are exact ISBN matches — selecting the same ISBN twice wastes a slot. Follett titles have no ISBN, so they show no auto-cover and use title-search links. Your selections are saved automatically in this browser — closing or reloading the page keeps them, and Clear asks for confirmation and offers an Undo, so you won't lose your picks by accident. Genre, age group, and page count come from Google Books / Open Library; many recent titles are sparsely catalogued, so some show “Unknown” — verify borderline calls.
